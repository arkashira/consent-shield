# Consent-Shield Product Requirements Document

## Problem Statement

The increasing adoption of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and technology in sensitive domains like health and education has raised significant concerns about data protection and consent management. Existing solutions often fall short in providing a comprehensive and user-centric approach to consent management, leading to non-compliance with regulations and erosion of trust among users.

## Target Users

* Data controllers and processors in sensitive domains like health and education
* Developers and engineers working on AI and technology projects
* Compliance officers and data protection officers responsible for ensuring regulatory compliance
* End-users who provide personal data for AI-driven services

## Goals

* Provide a robust and user-friendly consent management platform for AI and technology projects
* Ensure compliance with relevant regulations, such as GDPR and CCPA
* Enable data controllers and processors to demonstrate transparency and accountability in data handling
* Protect end-users' rights and interests in their personal data

## Key Features (Prioritized)

### Must-Haves

1. **Consent Management**: A user-friendly interface for collecting, storing, and managing user consent for data processing and sharing.
2. **Regulatory Compliance**: Integration with relevant regulations, such as GDPR and CCPA, to ensure compliance and provide audit trails.
3. **Data Protection**: Robust data protection measures, including encryption, access controls, and data minimization.
4. **Transparency**: Clear and concise communication of data processing activities and user rights.

### Nice-to-Haves

1. **Integration with AI and Technology Platforms**: Seamless integration with popular AI and technology platforms, such as TensorFlow and PyTorch.
2. **Automated Consent Requests**: Automated consent requests for data processing and sharing, reducing manual effort and improving user experience.
3. **Consent Analytics**: Insights and analytics on consent patterns and user behavior to inform data protection strategies.
4. **Multi-Language Support**: Support for multiple languages to cater to a global user base.

## Success Metrics

* Number of users onboarding with consent-shield
* Percentage of users providing explicit consent for data processing and sharing
* Number of compliance audits and certifications achieved
* User satisfaction ratings (e.g., Net Promoter Score)
* Revenue growth and customer acquisition costs

## Scope

* Development of the consent-shield platform, including user interface, data protection measures, and regulatory compliance features
* Integration with popular AI and technology platforms
* Multi-language support and automated consent requests
* Data analytics and insights for consent patterns and user behavior

## Out-of-Scope

* Development of AI and technology projects that require consent-shield
* Provision of data protection and compliance consulting services
* Integration with third-party data sources or platforms not explicitly listed
* Development of features not explicitly listed in the key features section

## Assumptions and Dependencies

* The consent-shield platform will be developed using a cloud-based infrastructure and scalable architecture.
* The platform will integrate with popular AI and technology platforms, such as TensorFlow and PyTorch.
* The platform will support multiple languages and provide automated consent requests.
* The platform will provide insights and analytics on consent patterns and user behavior.

## Risks and Mitigation Strategies

* Regulatory changes and updates may impact the platform's compliance features.
* User adoption and satisfaction may be affected by the platform's user experience and features.
* Data breaches or security incidents may compromise user data and trust.
* Competition from established players in the data protection and consent management market may impact market share and revenue growth.

By addressing these risks and assumptions, we can ensure the success and adoption of the consent-shield platform, providing a robust and user-friendly consent management solution for AI and technology projects in sensitive domains.
