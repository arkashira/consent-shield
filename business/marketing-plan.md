# Marketing Plan
## Positioning
Consent-shield: AI-powered consent and data protection platform for sensitive domains.
Alternative positionings:
1. **Data Guardian**: Comprehensive data protection and consent management for health and education.
2. **Consent Catalyst**: Streamlining consent and data protection for AI-driven innovation in sensitive sectors.
3. **Trust Anchor**: Establishing trust through robust consent and data protection solutions for AI and technology.

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
1. **Dr. Rachel Kim**, Health Informatics Specialist, $100,000 budget: Oversees data protection and compliance for a hospital network.
2. **Mark Davis**, Education Technology Director, $50,000 budget: Responsible for ensuring data privacy and security for a school district.
3. **Sofia Patel**, AI Ethics Consultant, $200,000 budget: Advises tech firms on ethical AI implementation, including consent and data protection.

## Channels
1. **LinkedIn Ads** (CAC: $500): Targeted ads for professionals in health, education, and AI ethics.
2. **Industry Conferences** (CAC: $2,000): Sponsorship and presentations at health, education, and AI conferences.
3. **Content Partnerships** (CAC: $1,000): Collaborations with industry thought leaders and publications.

## Content Cadence
- Week 1-2: **Problem Awareness** - Blog posts and social media highlighting consent and data protection challenges.
- Week 3-4: **Solution Introduction** - Introducing Consent-shield and its benefits through webinars and case studies.
- Week 5-6: **Deep Dive** - Technical whitepapers and demos showcasing Consent-shield's capabilities.
- Week 7-8: **Success Stories** - Customer testimonials and success stories.
- Week 9-12: **Thought Leadership** - Opinion pieces, interviews, and podcasts featuring Consent-shield experts.

## Launch Milestones
- **D-30**: Finalize marketing materials, launch website, and start LinkedIn Ads campaign.
- **D-0**: Official launch announcement, industry conference sponsorships begin.
- **D+30**: First content partnership published, start tracking user engagement metrics.
- **D+90**: Review success metrics, adjust marketing strategy as needed.

## Success Metrics
- **DAU (Daily Active Users)**: 500 by D+90.
- **$MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)**: $250,000 by D+90.
