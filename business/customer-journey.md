# Customer Journey Map
## Overview
The customer journey map for Consent Shield outlines the phases a potential customer goes through from initial awareness to expansion of the consent management platform. 

## Aware
* **Trigger Event**: Discovery of a data breach or privacy violation in their industry, prompting the need for robust consent and data protection measures.
* **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about available solutions, complexity of implementing new technology, perceived high costs.
* **User Emotions**: Concern, uncertainty, fear of non-compliance.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Educational content (blog posts, webinars) highlighting the importance and benefits of consent management, case studies of successful implementations.
* **Metric**: Website traffic, social media engagement, download of informational materials (e.g., whitepapers, eBooks).

## Consider
* **Trigger Event**: Identification of Consent Shield as a potential solution through research, recommendations, or marketing efforts.
* **Friction Points**: Comparing features and pricing with competitors, evaluating the ease of integration with existing systems.
* **User Emotions**: Hope, curiosity, skepticism about the effectiveness and compatibility of the platform.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Free trials or demos, detailed product comparisons, testimonials from similar businesses, responsive customer support.
* **Metric**: Trial sign-ups, demo requests, comparison page visits, customer support inquiries.

## Try
* **Trigger Event**: Decision to trial Consent Shield based on perceived value and potential fit.
* **Friction Points**: Technical difficulties during setup, uncertainty about how to fully utilize the platform's features.
* **User Emotions**: Excitement, anticipation, potential frustration if expectations are not met.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Seamless onboarding process, comprehensive documentation, responsive technical support, success stories from trial users.
* **Metric**: Trial conversion rates, user engagement metrics (e.g., feature adoption, time spent on the platform), support ticket resolution time.

## Adopt
* **Trigger Event**: Successful trial experience leading to the decision to purchase Consent Shield.
* **Friction Points**: Contract negotiations, payment processing, finalizing the implementation details.
* **User Emotions**: Relief, satisfaction, eagerness to fully integrate the platform.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Personalized onboarding, dedicated account management, regular check-ins to ensure satisfaction and address any concerns.
* **Metric**: Conversion from trial to paid customer, customer satisfaction surveys, net promoter score (NPS).

## Expand
* **Trigger Event**: Successful integration and positive experience with Consent Shield, leading to consideration of additional features or services.
* **Friction Points**: Evaluating the cost-benefit analysis of expanding the platform's use, ensuring that new features align with business needs.
* **User Emotions**: Loyalty, trust, anticipation of further benefits from the expanded use.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Proactive account management suggesting relevant additional services, loyalty programs, exclusive access to new features or beta testing.
* **Metric**: Upsell and cross-sell rates, customer retention rates, average revenue per user (ARPU) growth.

By understanding and addressing the needs, emotions, and pain points at each phase of the customer journey, Consent Shield can tailor its marketing, sales, and support efforts to provide a superior customer experience, ultimately driving business growth and customer loyalty.
